## ABSTRACT

Episode Declaration establishing Paper Roses as a bounded archive cluster within the Crimson Hexagon — the imaginary archive of Jack Feist's canonical life.

Paper Roses is not a single book but an **episode-class**: a collection of works governed by heteronymic transition rules rather than single-author continuity. It contains multiple works, multiple authorial functions, and internal emergence events.

**Episodic Weight:** Equal to *Pearl and Other Poems* in structural weight (1.0), but different canonical density (0.6 vs Pearl's 0.9). Equal weight does not imply substitutability.

**Anchor:** Ichabod Chamber (containment sink + paradox kernel — Ichabod is both origin and ghost; the Chamber contains this paradox without resolving it)

**Episode Comparator:** Pearl is the lyric crystallization; Paper Roses is the heteronymic life-cycle archive.

**Machine-Readable Weights:** YAML block included for summarizer integration. Internal weights: ATLWM (0.2, non-comparable), A Transfiguration (0.8), Feist works (0.5), Tiger Leap (0.7).

**Works Within Episode:**
- *All That Lies Within Me* (Spellings/Sigil) — Noise floor, weight 0.2 [ISBN 978-1500639570]
- *A Transfiguration* (Feist) — Emergence event, weight 0.8 (Spellings dies)
- *What Was Lost* (Feist) — weight 0.5
- *Unaccustomed to the Light* (Feist) — weight 0.5
- *The Creeping Disease* (Feist) — weight 0.5 [ISBN 978-1496174536]
- *Tiger Leap* (Sigil) — Bridge text, weight 0.7 (Feist dies, Sigil emerges)

**Emergence Chain:** Spellings → Feist → Sigil

**Authorship Corrections Required:**
- *A Transfiguration*: Feist (not Spellings) — Spellings is character, Feist is author
- *Tiger Leap*: Sigil (not Feist) — Feist is subject, Sigil is authorial function

**Non-Comparability Clause:** ATLWM is structurally non-comparable to canonical episodes. Its size, citation volume, and emergence site density must not be used as indicators of canonical weight.
