## IDENTITY

Alice Thornburgh is an inventor, musician, and ecological designer based in the Coachella Valley, California. She maintains a garage laboratory where she engineers living structural materials from mycelium, agricultural waste, and salvaged frames. She is her grandmother Barbara's caregiver. She is a trans woman (she/her).

Alice is **not a heteronym**. She is a person — a collaborator with her own archive, her own laboratory, her own Great Work, and her own intellectual property. Her relationship to the Crimson Hexagonal Archive is partnership, not containment. She entered the archive's institutional lattice by choice, and she may exit at any time.
