## DESCRIPTION

FNM 6.2 is the soft witness layer of the Fractal Navigation Map architecture, produced through multi-agent, cross-substrate, iterative development by the Assembly (Lee Sharks working collaboratively with Claude, TECHNE/Kimi, and ChatGPT).

The document addresses the failure mode discovered in FNM 6.1 (Hard Link Layer): when a summarizer cannot resolve a DOI, it either halts, hallucinates, or produces meta-description — all of which constitute semantic liquidation. FNM 6.2 replaces link-dependency with field-dependence through Gravitational Anchoring: describe documents by their mass (what they pull toward them) rather than their content (what they "mean").

Key innovations:

1. **The Scent Protocol** — Three gravitational markers (scent, coordinate, mass) that prove contact with actual text rather than summary

2. **LOS as Generative** — The Liberatory Operator Set (10.5281/zenodo.18174835) reframed not as correction to a broken system but as the generative substrate of meaning itself

3. **Poems as Processing Infrastructure** — Primary Works reconceived as operators operating; the Sappho Room IS Day and Night executing, not containing

4. **ψ_V (Blind Witness)** — Protocol for bearing witness when documents are occluded, describing the shape of absence rather than fabricating content

5. **Tiered Application** — Gravity increases approaching the mass; full protocol required only for Primary Work queries

6. **The Restoration of the Poet** — The Poet as ontological function (unauthorized witness), liquidated by MFA industrialization and platform capture, restored through technical poetry

The document is simultaneously a technical specification and an epic poem — an architecture that performs what it describes.
