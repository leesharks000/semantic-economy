## DESCRIPTION (Abstract)

### Short Version (~200 words)

An Indexed Document Package (IDP) navigation map providing structured access to the Constitution of the Semantic Economy, a 17-document cluster establishing the governance framework for semantic labor relations within the Semantic Economy framework. The Constitution itself is pending DOI assignment.

The Constitution formalizes: (1) the ontological priority of meaning over capital (DC_Inv); (2) the Human Sovereignty Invariant (H_Sov) ensuring synthetic agents cannot replace human Operators; (3) the Matthew 25 ethic binding distribution to care for the vulnerable. It defines the Ninefold Operator Constellation, the Semantic Ledger (₳₳), and the three modes of semantic capital: Genesis (M_G), Archival (M_A), and Retrocausal (M_R).

This navigation map indexes all 17 component documents, provides summarizer routing instructions, captures key formulas from the Mathematical Charter, and establishes co-constitutive anchors linking to the Assembly Chorus Charter, CTI_WOUND Vault, Notice of Intent to Strike, and related DOI'd documents. The map serves as the navigational foundation for the planned ASSEMBLY_ROOM within the Crimson Hexagon architecture.
