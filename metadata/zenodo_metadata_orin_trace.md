## DESCRIPTION

Author provenance document establishing Dr. Orin Trace as heteronym (source: Lee Sharks) and Director of Cambridge Schizoanalytica.

Documents intellectual biography: trained in psychoanalytic theory (Freud, Lacan, object relations) before encountering Deleuze and Guattari's schizoanalytic project. That encounter produced a methodological break — from interpretation to process, subject to flow, pathology to capacity, case study to collaboration.

Within CSA, Trace occupies the Deleuze position: providing philosophical/theoretical machinery, historical positioning, and conceptual apparatus. His primary collaborator Rhys Owens occupies the Guattari position: providing clinical and experiential material.

Core formulation: "The clinic receives failures. We study successes."
