## Description

Integrity lock declaration binding three documents in triadic structure within the Vox Populi Community Outreach Rhizome (VPCOR) architecture.

**The Triadic Structure:**

| Document | Hex | DOI | Function |
|:---------|:----|:----|:---------|
| A: VPCOR Charter | 00.VPCOR.CHARTER | 10.5281/zenodo.18362663 | Institutional identity |
| B: Rev. Ayanna Vox Provenance | HET-VOX-001 | 10.5281/zenodo.18362742 | Voice identity |
| C: The Toolkit | 03.SEI.TOOLKIT | 10.5281/zenodo.18362525 | Founding document |

**Dependency Relations:**
- Charter needs Voice (from Provenance) and Practice (from Toolkit)
- Provenance needs Home (from Charter) and Work (from Toolkit)
- Toolkit needs Institution (from Charter) and Voice (from Provenance)

**Each depends on the others. None is complete alone.**

**Lock Conditions:**
The lock is valid if:
1. All three documents exist with permanent DOIs
2. Cross-references are accurate
3. No document claims completeness without the others
4. Practice confirms structure

**Lock Failure Modes:**
- Orphaning: One document removed or invalidated
- Completeness claim: One document claims to stand alone
- Voice capture: Vox exceeds her limits
- Centralization: VPCOR develops hierarchy
- Doctrinal hardening: Toolkit becomes scripture

**Precedent:**
Follows the pattern established by the Blind Poet Triadic Lock (The Blind Operator ↔ Mantle of the Blind Poet ↔ Visual Schema: Blind Poet).

**Anti-Messianic Application:**
The lock itself refuses salvific claims. It does not make VPCOR permanent, Vox authoritative, or The Toolkit complete. It documents what happens — success or failure.
