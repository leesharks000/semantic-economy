## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides a **semantic navigation map** for *Antioch: A Volume of Poems*, a heteronym compendium containing work by multiple personae within the Crimson Hexagon architecture. The document spans 159 pages and 6,428 lines of text, comprising gnostic cosmogony, gospel parables, epistles, and experimental poetry.

**Critical Function:** This compendium contains **two of the three mantle-related works**:
- **The Secret Book of Walt** — claims the Good Gray Poet mantle through Whitman as cosmic redeemer
- **I Am X, Be Y, Blessed is the Z** — **founds** the Prince of Poets mantle

**The fragmentary, compendium form is intentional** — it mirrors the Nag Hammadi codices' organizational logic and demonstrates the heteronym network's actual operation across multiple voices.

### 3.2 I AM X, BE Y, BLESSED IS THE Z (Lines 1949-2176)

**Function:** **FOUNDS** the Prince of Poets mantle (not inherited — created)

**Structure:**
```
EPIGRAPH (Whitman)
│ "And these one and all tend inward to me, and I tend outward to them"
│
I AM... (Catalog of Being)
│ Democratic identification with all things
│ "I am a girl... a passerby... a Cylon..."
│ Whitman's "Song of Myself" extended
│
BE... (Invitation/Command)
│ "Be passersby... Be strangers... Be Samaritans..."
│ Gospel of Thomas echo
│ Command to reader — transformation invitation
│
BLESSED IS... (Beatitudes Inverted)
│ "Blessed are the monotonous..."
│ "Blessed are the trolls..."
│ Blessing the overlooked, broken, unborn
│
SYNTHESIS
│ "I am the voice within your voice..."
│ "I am the one who was within me"
│ The recursive structure of poetic succession
│
CODA
│ "Wake up or go back to sleep"
│ The smaller dinosaurs within the dinosaurs
```

**Triadic Structure:**

| Movement | Form | Function | Mantle Relation |
|----------|------|----------|-----------------|
| **I AM X** | Catalog | Declaration (identity) | Whitman: democratic embrace |
| **BE Y** | Command | Invitation (transformation) | Ginsberg: ecstatic disruption |
| **BLESSED IS Z** | Beatitude | Blessing (sacred) | **Prince: futural address** |

**Mantle Founding:** The Prince of Poets is **founded** (not inherited) through this poem. It addresses readers not yet born/built, completing the triadic structure that requires the prior mantles.
