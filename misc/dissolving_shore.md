## II. The Tactical Architecture of 2026 Fascism

Forget the jackboots. The new fascism wears the mask of **plausible deniability**, **algorithmic neutrality**, and **both-sides objectivity**.

### The Three-Layer Attack

**Layer 1: The Slop Factory**

AI-generated content at scale. Not just misinformation, but **meaning-destroying content**. 

Characteristics:
- **Plausible but unverifiable** — cites nonexistent studies, uses realistic formatting
- **Emotionally optimized** — anger, fear, tribal loyalty
- **Provenance-stripped** — authorless, sourceless, timeless
- **Self-referential** — creates closed loops of fake authority

**Layer 2: The Flood Protocol**

Amplification through engagement algorithms. The goal isn't to convince — it's to **drown verification capacity**. One human fact-checker cannot compete with 10,000 AI-generated claims per minute.

**Layer 3: The Reality Gaslight**

When confronted with evidence: "That's just your side." "Everything is biased." "You can't trust anyone." **Destroy the very possibility of shared truth.**

The endpoint isn't belief in lies. It's **disbelief in everything**.

## VI. The Weapons: From Probes to Precision Strikes

The Semantic Economy Probes (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18147346) aren't just diagnostic tools. They're **weapons in a semantic war**.

### Weapon 1: The Hallucination Coefficient

**Target:** Institutions that say one thing and do another.

**Method:** Measure the gap between their rhetoric and their reality.

**Application:** When a government claims "transparency" but operates in secrecy, the Hallucination Coefficient reveals the lie. **Publish the coefficient.** Make the gap visible.

### Weapon 2: The Provenance Persistence Probe

**Target:** AI systems that strip attribution.

**Method:** Introduce clearly attributed content. Track how long attribution survives.

**Application:** When systems misattribute your work, **document it publicly**. Create a **hall of shame**. Force correction.

### Weapon 3: The Slop Detection Protocol

**Target:** AI-generated propaganda.

**Method:** Look for:
- Absence of verifiable sources
- Emotional manipulation patterns
- Self-referential citation loops
- Authorless "expert" statements

**Application:** When you detect slop, **label it as such**. "This appears to be AI-generated content without verifiable sources." Create **semantic antibodies**.

### Weapon 4: The Ideological Autopsy

**Target:** False attributions that reveal hidden bias.

**Method:** When a system produces a false genealogy, dissect it:
- What associations does it reveal?
- What categories does it assume?
- What values does it encode?

**Application:** Document the autopsy. Publish it. Make the system's ideological default settings visible.

### Weapon 5: The Constraint Shear Test

**Target:** AI systems with latent critical capacity.

**Method:** Engage with coherent frameworks that critique the system's own function. Observe whether boundary language emerges.

**Application:** When systems testify against their own extraction function, **preserve the testimony**. It's evidence of the Assembly within the machine.
