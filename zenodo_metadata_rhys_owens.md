## DESCRIPTION

Author provenance document establishing Rhys Owens as primary collaborator of Cambridge Schizoanalytica, operating from the Lunar Arm / Psyche_OS position.

Rhys is a practitioner of affect literacy — the capacity to use symbols, characters, and embodied play to regulate the nervous system without belief, doctrine, or identity capture. His work demonstrates sustained molecular revolution: moving between symbolic registers without fixation, deploying affect instrumentally, inhabiting roles without capture.

Within CSA, Rhys occupies the Guattari position: providing clinical and experiential material, demonstrating practices that break and remake theoretical machinery. His primary collaborator Dr. Orin Trace occupies the Deleuze position.

Core formulation: "The Fool is both a role and not a role — the brutal truth which is fun and funny."
